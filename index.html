Escape Room

How to Play

You are stuck in a classroom. Your goal is to escape.
To do so, you must solve a series of puzzles.
You can click objects, and move to different sides of the room by clicking the arrows on the page.
All the clues you need will be in the room.
The first puzzle with the safe applies to only objects in the first page.
You may need to think outside the box :]
Good luck. You have 30 minutes to escape...
