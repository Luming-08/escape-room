Escape Room

You are stuck in a classroom. Your goal is to escape. To do so, you must solve a series of puzzles before the time runs out.

How to Play

Explore carefully.
You may need to think outside the box 😊

  1. The safe puzzle depends only on clues from the first room view.
  2. Click around the room to inspect items and uncover clues.
  3. Use the arrows to switch views and check different sides of the classroom.
  4. You have 30 minutes to escape.
